The legal advice mobile app allows clients to directly search for lawyers based on location, specialty, ratings, reviews, and other keywords. Clients can enter their search criteria such as "family lawyer in Lalitpur" or "patent attorney with 5 star reviews".

The app will display a list of lawyers matching the keywords along with their profiles, experience, ratings, reviews, fees, and more. Clients can go through the lawyer profiles and select ones they wish to contact or book appointments with directly through the app. The search functionality gives clients an easy way to find and connect with lawyers that fit their needs
