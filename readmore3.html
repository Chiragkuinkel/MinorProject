Welcome

Clients who are new to the app can get lawyer recommendations by entering their unique requirements instead of past appointment data. They can specify legal needs, specialty required, budget, location and other preferences. The app's powerful recommendation algorithm will analyze this information and suggest the top matching lawyers.

This customized approach allows new clients to get tailored recommendations that fit their scenario. The app takes into factors like legal complexity, specialized expertise and cost sensitivity when suggesting lawyers. The requirements-based search ensures clients get recommendations perfectly aligned with their needs.
